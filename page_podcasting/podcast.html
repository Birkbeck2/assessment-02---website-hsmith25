Podcasting

In 2019, I began my own podcast, titled That 90s Spider-Man Show, where I, along with another host, discuss/make fun of episodes of Spider-Man: The Animated Series, talk about upcoming Marvel projects, and occassionally dive into the very confusing world of Spider-Man comics.

So, what do I do?

Primarily, I'm the co-host, but being part of the podcast has given me podcast production and audio editing skills (both with Adobe Audition and Audacity). I also do some video editing for the YouTube channel, using Adobe Premiere.

Have a listen!
